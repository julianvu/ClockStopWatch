Given the angle of rotation, we recalculate the x and y coordinates of the ends of the clockhand.

Calculating angle of rotation: pi/2 since angle is from 0 on unit circle.
Subtracting pi/2 from angle of rotation will rotate the clock counter-clockwise by a quarter radian.
